Outreach

Our team has strong ties with our community, whether local or global. Annually, we help over 500,000 people through the lessons we create, workshops we teach, tournaments we host, etc. Despite it being our first year in FIRST Robotics Competition team, we continue the traditions we began in FIRST LEGO League of being actively involved in the FIRST, MINDSTORMS, STEM and local communities. Our goal is not only to spread FIRST, but also to inspire students to take part in STEM. We want to expose our community to new ideas and new opportunities.
