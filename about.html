Our students and mentors

This is our rookie season in FIRST Robotics Competition, but we are not new to FIRST. Most of the team has had prior experience in FIRST LEGO League and FIRST Tech Challenge. We are a combination of students from three award-winning FIRST LEGO League teams in Western Pennsylvania. We are students from three school districts and in 9th through 12th grades. We are based in Sharon, Pennsylvania.
