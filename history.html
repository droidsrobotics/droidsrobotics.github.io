FRC 8027

Droids Robotics supports FRC 8027 based in Sharon, PA. They are a merger between three area FIRST LEGO League Challenge Teams with a ten year history in FIRST.

April 2020: FIRST World Championships Detroit, MI
March 2020: Greater Pittsburgh Regional (Cancelled) California, PA
March 2020: Rookie All Star (Qualified for Worlds) Dayton, Ohio
September 2019: Finalist Alliance MVRC Warren, Ohio
May 2019: FIRST Robotics Competition Team is registered!
